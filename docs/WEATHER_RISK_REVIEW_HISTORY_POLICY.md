# Weather Risk Review History Policy

M80 adds local reviewer history fixtures for weather risk governance QA.

History events include:

- reviewer assigned
- reviewer changed
- safety reviewer pending
- rule reverted
- stale review
- release blocked

These are placeholders only. They do not represent real expert approval and do not create production rule activation.

Stale reviews must warn the UI and block release until refreshed by a future real reviewer workflow.
