# Weather Risk Safety Policy

Weather risk intelligence in KasetHub is general caution only.

## Allowed

- Explain that rain or wind may affect spraying conditions.
- Explain that high humidity may be a reason to inspect crops more carefully.
- Explain that high heat may require planning outdoor work carefully.
- Explain that stale forecasts should be checked again.
- Suggest checking local conditions and official weather sources.
- Suggest asking a local expert when uncertain.

## Blocked

- Product or sponsor recommendation.
- Chemical product recommendation.
- Exact chemical or fertilizer dose.
- Instructions that override labels.
- Guaranteed yield, profit, safety, or drying result.
- Crop-specific disease diagnosis.
- Push notification automation without future review.

## Required Disclaimers

- `คำแนะนำเบื้องต้น ไม่แทนผู้เชี่ยวชาญ`
- `อ่านฉลากจริงก่อนใช้เสมอ`
- `พยากรณ์อากาศอาจคลาดเคลื่อนได้`

## Data Boundary

M78 uses weather adapter data only. It does not require GPS, precise farm location, Supabase writes, backend writes, or cloud sync.
