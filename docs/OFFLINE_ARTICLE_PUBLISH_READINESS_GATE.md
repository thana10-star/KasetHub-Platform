# Offline Article Publish Readiness Gate

M67 keeps all full-content article templates blocked from `ready_for_full_publish`.

## Required Before Publish

- Source placeholders must be filled with reviewed references.
- Required safety disclaimers must remain present.
- Reviewer placeholders must be completed.
- Last reviewed date must be set.
- Planned image metadata must be valid.
- Finance, government, or fast-changing content must include a freshness date.
- Fertilizer or chemical-adjacent content must include the real-label warning.
- Risky topics must include expert escalation notes.

## Current M67 Status

All pilot templates are `draft_template`. They are designed to show blockers clearly, not to publish full article content.

## CMS Rule

Future CMS content may extend article bodies, but it cannot remove required disclaimers or bypass the publish gate.
