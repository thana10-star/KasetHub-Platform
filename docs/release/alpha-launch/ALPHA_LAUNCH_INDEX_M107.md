# Alpha Launch Index M107

## Purpose

This folder is the owner-facing launch pack for the V1 Cloudflare/PWA alpha preview. It helps the owner share the app with selected testers, collect feedback, and decide what to fix before any Android wrapper work.

This pack does not submit to any store, implement a wrapper, enable AI providers, enable cloud sync, add GPS, or change app behavior.

## Who Should Test First

- owner and internal reviewers
- trusted farmers or agriculture-interested users
- selected YouTube audience members who understand this is an early preview
- people using real mobile phones, especially Android phones

## What To Test

- Home clarity
- Ask AI page usefulness and safety copy
- Weather live display and readability
- calculator/tool usability
- My Farm and Farm Records Basic Mode clarity
- Help/Profile and support/privacy discoverability
- mobile readability, spacing, and navigation

## What Not To Test

- production AI provider reliability if the provider is still disabled or limited
- cloud sync, account sync, or backup
- GPS or precise location
- receipt upload, OCR, camera diagnosis, or notifications
- Android/iOS wrapper behavior
- official accounting, legal, weather-warning, or agronomy guarantee workflows

## Feedback Collection Method

Recommended default:

- Google Form for structured alpha feedback

- support email if the owner prefers direct messages and can manage the inbox
