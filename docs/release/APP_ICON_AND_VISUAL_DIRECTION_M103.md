# App Icon And Visual Direction M103

## Purpose

This document gives the owner a simple visual direction for V1 release assets. It is not a final brand system and does not implement new app UI.

## App Icon Concept

Core idea:

- green leaf
- subtle AI spark
- simple farm field

The icon should feel Thai agriculture friendly, modern, and trustworthy. It should read clearly at small sizes on a phone home screen and in app store search results.

## Visual Principles

- Keep the silhouette simple.
- Use one strong central symbol instead of many small details.
- Make the leaf or field readable at 48px and 32px.
- Use enough contrast against light and dark device backgrounds.
- Avoid small text inside the icon.
- Avoid official/government-looking seals.
- Avoid fake certification marks.
- Avoid pesticide, chemical bottle, warning skull, or disease imagery.
- Avoid overloading the icon with crop photos, tool icons, weather icons, and AI symbols all at once.

## Possible Color Palette

- Green for agriculture and growth.
- Warm yellow for sunlight and helpfulness.
- Soil brown for farm grounding.
- White or cream for clean contrast.

Suggested balance:

- Primary: rich natural green.
- Accent: warm yellow AI spark or sunrise.
- Secondary: restrained soil brown field lines.
- Background: clean white/cream or deep green.

## Store Screenshot Visual Direction

- Use real app screens with Thai text.
- Keep captions short and practical.
- Do not add heavy marketing frames that hide the actual app.
- Prefer clean phone screenshots that show the UI clearly.
- Keep the app promise practical: ask, check weather, calculate, and record.

## Final Asset Notes

- Export final icon in store-required sizes after wrapper/release channel is chosen.
- Verify the icon at small sizes before approving.
- Confirm the icon does not imply official status, certification, or guaranteed expert output.
