# App Icon Final Brief M105

## Purpose

This brief finalizes the V1 app icon direction for owner review and future asset production. It does not generate image files or implement app UI changes.

M106 final approval tracker: `docs/release/v1-package/APP_ICON_APPROVAL_STATUS_M106.md`.

## Final Icon Concept

The V1 icon should combine:

- a green leaf as the primary agriculture signal
- an AI spark or subtle digital helper cue
- a farm field or soil base for practical farm grounding
- a simple centered shape that stays readable at small sizes

The icon should feel like a friendly Thai agriculture utility: modern, clear, and helpful without looking official, certified, or overly technical.

## Must Avoid

- official government seal look
- fake certification badge or approval mark
- pesticide, chemical bottle, skull, or warning-label imagery
- too many tiny details that disappear at phone icon size
- cluttered Thai text inside the icon
- realistic crop photo collage
- strong emergency-warning styling that implies official alerts

## Recommended Palette

- Deep green: primary icon shape and agriculture identity.
- Fresh leaf green: leaf highlight and growth cue.
- Warm yellow highlight: AI spark, sunrise, or helpful assistant cue.
- Soil brown: restrained field/soil base detail.
- Cream or white background: clean contrast and store readability.

Recommended balance:

- keep green dominant
- use yellow only as a small helper/spark accent
- keep brown subtle and low in the composition
- test on both light and dark phone backgrounds
