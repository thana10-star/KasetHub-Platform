# App Icon Approval Status M106

## Purpose

This tracker locks the final V1 icon direction and records owner approval/export status. It does not add icon image files.

## Final Concept Summary

The V1 icon should combine:

- green leaf
- AI spark or subtle digital helper cue
- farm field / soil base
- simple centered composition readable at small sizes

Avoid government seal styling, fake certification badges, pesticide/chemical imagery, tiny clutter, and Thai text inside the icon.

Square mobile app icon for "KasetHub", simple modern green leaf above subtle farm field lines, small warm yellow AI spark, deep green and fresh leaf green palette, soil brown base detail, cream or white background, centered composition, high contrast, readable at 32px, no text, no government seal, no certification badge, no pesticide or chemical imagery, polished app-store-ready icon.
