# Offline Article Full Content Template

M67 prepares draft templates for future full offline agriculture articles. These templates are not final official articles and must not be treated as reviewed agronomy or finance guidance.

## Template Blocks

Each pilot template supports:

- intro
- key points
- practical steps
- calculation/tool links
- common mistakes
- safety warnings
- local context notes
- when to ask expert
- summary
- related app actions

## Pilot Templates

- `soil-types-before-planting`
- `npk-label-basics`
- `rice-cost-profit-per-rai`
- `cassava-plant-spacing-per-rai`
- `farm-break-even-price`

Some pilot IDs map to existing bundled offline fallback articles until a dedicated full article body exists.

## Boundaries

- No AI article generation.
- No official finance, loan, government, fertilizer, or crop-dose facts are hardcoded.
- No external image or CDN dependency.
- Offline fallback articles remain available even after future CMS content exists.
