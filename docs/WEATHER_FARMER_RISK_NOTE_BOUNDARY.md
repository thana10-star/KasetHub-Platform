# Weather Farmer Risk Note Boundary

M76 adds general farmer-friendly weather notes for spraying, wind, rain, and forecast uncertainty.

## Required Notes

- `ก่อนพ่นยาให้ดูฝนและลม`
- `ลมแรงอาจทำให้ละอองยาปลิว`
- `ฝนหลังพ่นยาอาจลดประสิทธิภาพ`
- `พยากรณ์อากาศอาจคลาดเคลื่อนได้`

## Boundaries

Risk notes must remain general. They must not:

- recommend a chemical product
- prescribe a pesticide dose
- define exact legal wind thresholds
- override real product labels
- guarantee yield, profit, safety, or timing
- replace local expert or official weather review

## Detail Rule

When a note mentions spraying, it must remind users to check the real label and local field conditions. This keeps weather guidance useful without becoming hidden agronomy or chemical advice.
