# Offline Article Expert Escalation Notes

M67 marks risky full-article topics with expert escalation requirements.

## Escalation Areas

- Fertilizer or chemical safety
- Finance, loan, government, or price-sensitive decisions
- Yield or profit expectation
- Crop health and disease-sensitive guidance
- Local soil, water, and seasonal conditions

## Required Behavior

Risky articles must tell readers when to ask a local expert or official agency. Expert escalation notes must remain visible in the readiness gate before any future full publish status is allowed.

## Safety Boundary

The article system may explain basic concepts, but it must not replace local professional advice, real labels, soil tests, or official finance documents.
