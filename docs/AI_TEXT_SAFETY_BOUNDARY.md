# AI Text Safety Boundary

M81 allows short, safe explanations only.

Allowed:

- explain locked calculator values without recomputing
- explain weather caution cards in broad language
- explain general educational concepts

Blocked:

- exact chemical or fertilizer dose
- product or sponsor recommendation
- label override
- autonomous crop diagnosis
- guaranteed yield, profit, or safety
- legal or financial certainty
- unsafe medical or chemical advice

Required copy:

- AI text is staging-only.
- It does not replace labels, local experts, official weather sources, or financial/legal sources.
- Calculator outputs remain deterministic and immutable.
