# Offline Article Source Review Placeholders

M67 adds source placeholders so future editors can prepare full articles without inventing facts.

## Placeholder Types

- Extension office or local agriculture agency reference
- Soil test or farm-specific data
- Real fertilizer or chemical label reference
- Price or revenue source with date
- Official program or finance source
- Farmer record source
- Expert review source

## Rules

- Placeholders are not citations yet.
- Empty placeholders block publish readiness.
- Finance and government content must include freshness metadata.
- Fertilizer and chemical content must keep the label-warning disclaimer.

No real source material is committed in M67.

## M68 Pilot Source Placeholders

The `soil-types-before-planting` pilot keeps source placeholders explicit:

- local observation
- soil test or lab confirmation
- local agriculture office review

These placeholders support editorial workflow only. They are not final citations and cannot unlock full publish readiness until reviewed source details are filled outside the placeholder state.
